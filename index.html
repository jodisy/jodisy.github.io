🐡 About Me

Hey! I'm Jodi Sy & I'm a 16 year old from Toronto currently on explore mode. My passions are ever-evolving but right now I'm super interested in entrepreneurship, product, and biotechnology. I've gained a lot of valuable outreach experience in the nonprofit space which I hope to leverage as I actively seek opportunities to work at mission-driven startups. In my spare time, I like drinking boba with my friends and making art!

🍮 What I've worked on




Cell Senescence

I researched how the buildup of senescent cells causes aging and created a video on this topic for the 2020 Breakthrough Junior Challenge. My video placed in the top 10% of all submissions worldwide.



President @ United Under Arts Toronto

During August, we hosted 12 online workshops that guided 135+ students towards learning a new art hobby or honing their craft. Currently, we're creating personalized playlists for senior homes and curating creative inspiration for Gen-Z.



Founder & Exec. Director @ Probe

What started off as a side project has turned into a team of 20+ high school students working to demystify the major forces in science & technology shaping the world today. Through educational illustrations, virtual events, and videos, my team & I strive to make scientific innovation more accessible to Gen Z.



Sketchbook Tour 2020

Take a quick peek through my sketchbook in this fun 1 min video :)



Stock Market Landscapes

Inspired by the work of Gladys Estolas, I decided to hone my design skills by transforming otherwise dull stock market charts into pretty landscapes because people are inspired by beauty & wonder, not facts & figures. The above illustration depicts TWST from January-August.



Illustrator @ Dear Asian Youth

I illustrate social media posts and stickers for Dear Asian Youth to increase political engagement through education and to raise funds for the Black Lives Matter Movement.
